Personal Information

Communication is a central concept in social psychology that refers to the process by which individuals exchange messages, information, and ideas with one another. It is the means by which people form and maintain relationships, shape attitudes and beliefs, and influence the behavior of others. In social psychology, the study of communication is concerned with understanding the many factors that influence how messages are created, transmitted, received, and interpreted. This includes the role of nonverbal cues, emotions, and technology in shaping communication, as well as the impact of social norms, attitudes, and beliefs on the communication process.
